John Salter

John Salter is the lead developer of Now We're Cooking. He has a passion for all things game, web, and mobile development. When he isn't working on a game/website/app he can be found enjoying life with his girlfriend. Some of his intrests include running, video games, design, fashion, craft beer, and reading the news.

Antonio Lee

Antonio Lee is a game and web developer/designer. He uses his skills in art, music and programming to create new forms of playable and interactive media. Antonio learns quickly and likes to help in all aspects of development when possible. He loves food, nature and friendly people.

Ryan Gardner

Ryan Gardner is a game designer and a musician. He has a BA in Music Industry and Technology from CSU Chico and is currently working towards a BS in Computer Science: Computer Game Design from UC Santa Cruz. He enjoys designing games that find inovative ways of adding music into the player experience.

Jasmine Sien

Jasmine Sien loves programming and finding ways to create UI/UX for games. She uses her creativity to come up with designs and has an eye for detail in art and animation. Besides programming, Jasmine has a passion for art(drawing and sketching), sewing, making costumes, web designing and photography. Most importantly, cats are Jasmine's best friends.
